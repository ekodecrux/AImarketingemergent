1. **Autonomous, not assistive** — daily Co-Pilot fires Slack/WhatsApp briefs and auto-schedules recovery content

3. **Indian payment-native** — Razorpay + UPI + WhatsApp + INR pricing (₹499/₹999) where competitors charge ₹3-15K/mo
4. **Real, not mocked** — production Meta Graph publishing, real LinkedIn/X/Facebook/Instagram posting, real Hunter+AI lead enrichment
5. **Encrypted-at-rest from day one** — every OAuth token Fernet AES-128
